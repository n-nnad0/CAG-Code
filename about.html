Our Story

TrustSend was founded in 2024 by Nur Niki Nadhila and her dedicated team. Inspired by a TEDTalk, the group of youths set out to address the challenges foreign wrokers are currently facing: sending money to their loved ones across borders. TrustSend was created to provide a more affordable and secure remittance service for all.

Our Values

Transparency

We believe that there should not be any hidden fees, as users deserve to know exactly what they are paying for. We want to ensure customers can trust our services.

Customer-Centricity

Users here are our sole purpose in creating this website. Our services are user-friendly and simple, ensuring that every interaction is clear.

Our Technology

TrustSend leverages blockchain technology to ensure secure, transparent, and efficient transactions. By utilizing smart contracts and distributed ledger technology, we're able to provide a level of security and traceability that traditional systems can't match.
